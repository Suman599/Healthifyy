How it works

Your daily dose of health in simple 3 steps

01

Tell us what you like (and what not)

Firstly, we need to know your dietary preferences. Then we can customise your diet which meets your goal without compromising on taste and nutrition. With Healthifyy you can be sure that the food you consume is full of nutrition and you can have them guilt free.

02

Tell us what time you like to eat

In this step we need to know how many times you would like to consume your food and also the time you eat. For example, if you eat 2 times a day, then both times you have to eat food which will be filling such that you dont get hungry within few hours of consuming your food. So, we need to know how many times a day you eat your food and then leave it upon us to healthify your food.

03

In the third step we will calculate how much calories you need per day.

Once we know about your calorie intake per day we will customise your diet and according to that we will divide your food into 2 or 3 slots a day as you mentioned in the previous step. We will allow your to choose from an array of dishes which fits in well with your dietary preference and you goal.
